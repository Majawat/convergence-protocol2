No 1" proximity (unless charging). No moving through units.

Shooting

Pick 1 target (or 2 if multi-weapon). Models with LoS & range fire. Sum Attacks. Quality tests for hits. Target Defense tests to block. Remove casualties.

Melee

Chargers to base contact. Defenders move 3" to engage. Models in 2" horiz / 4" vert strike. Defenders strike back.
Fatigue: After 1st attack (charge/strike back), hits on 6s in melee this round.

Morale

If unit <= half models/Tough OR loses melee: Quality test. Fail = Shaken.
Shaken: Idle, Fatigued, auto-fail Morale, no objectives. Idle to recover.
Routed (Melee Loss at <= Half Str): Unit removed.

Terrain

Cover: +1 Def vs Shoot. Difficult: Max move 6". Dangerous: Test on entry/activation; '1' = 1 wound.

Primary Objective:

Control the "AI Objective" at game end to win. If AI Objective is destroyed, ALL PLAYERS LOSE.

Deployment:

Deployment: Chosen quarter as usual, >= 9" from center.

Special Rule: NPC Spawning

Start of each round, spawn NPCs at Dimensional Portal:

  • Round 1: 1 Rift Spawn, 1 Rift Horror
  • Round 2: 2 Rift Spawns, 1 Rift Horror
  • Round 3: 2 Rift Spawns, 1 Rift Horror, 1 Rift Monstrosity
  • Round 4+: ALL Hell Breaks Loose
NPC Behavior:

NPCs activate at end of every player turn. Move toward AI Objective. If in contact with AI, attack it. Otherwise, attack player unit with least wounds.

Victory:

Control AI Objective at game end wins. If AI destroyed, everyone loses.
