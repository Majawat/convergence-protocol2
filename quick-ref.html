Core Rules (GF v3.4.1 Summary)

Activation & Movement
Action Move Notes
Hold 0" Can shoot
Advance 6" Can shoot after move
Rush 12" Can't shoot
Charge 12" To melee, ignore 1" rule

No 1" proximity (unless charging). No moving through units.

Shooting

Pick 1 target (or 2 if multi-weapon). Models with LoS & range fire. Sum Attacks. Quality tests for hits. Target Defense tests to block. Remove casualties.

Melee

Chargers to base contact. Defenders move 3" to engage. Models in 2" horiz / 4" vert strike. Defenders strike back.
Fatigue: After 1st attack (charge/strike back), hits on 6s in melee this round.

Morale

If unit <= half models/Tough OR loses melee: Quality test. Fail = Shaken.
Shaken: Idle, Fatigued, auto-fail Morale, no objectives. Idle to recover.
Routed (Melee Loss at <= Half Str): Unit removed.

Terrain

Cover: +1 Def vs Shoot. Difficult: Max move 6". Dangerous: Test on entry/activation; '1' = 1 wound.

Primary Objective:

Sole control of central "Axiom Confluence" at end of Round 4 wins. Contested: most models win. Tie: draw.

Deployment & XP:

Deployment:Chosen quarter as ususal, >= 9" from center.

XP: End Rounds 1-3, unit with sole control of Axiom (survived Event) gets +D3 XP.

Special Rule: Axiom Confluence Event

Start of Rounds 1-4 (after init, before 1st turn). Controller (or 1st player if none/contested) rolls D6 for what warrior is deployed

  • (Warrior Deployed): Ancient Warrior unit spawns (see stats in 'Spawned Warrior Units' card below). Roller picks type (D6):
    • 1-3: Elite Construct-Sorcerer
    • 4: Unstable Automaton
    • 5: Containment Wardens (x3)
    • 6 (Wild Surge): Unit takes D3 hits (AP0)
  • Spawn: Within 1" of next Stasis Pod (1-4, once per pod). If all used, no more warriors.
  • Control: Activates immediately (by player to left of event roller). Hostile to Axiom controller (or closest). Then neutral, attacks nearest end of rounds.
Victory:

Sole control of Axiom Confluence (end of Round 4) wins.
